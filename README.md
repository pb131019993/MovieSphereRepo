App Name: MovieSphere
Purpose: The app allows users to browse popular people (actors, directors, etc.) from the movie industry, search for specific individuals, and view their details along with a gallery of images.
Key Features:
1. Display a list of popular people with infinite scrolling.
2. Search for people with infinite scrolling.
3. View detailed information about a person, including their images in a grid layout.

Architecture: MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel)
Dependencies:
Retrofit, Glide, ViewModel and LiveData, Coroutine etc.
